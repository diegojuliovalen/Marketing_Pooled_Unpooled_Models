## Marketing Analytics: Promotional Effectiveness in the Deodorant Market

This project was developed as part of the **MSc in Business Analytics (BI Oslo)** to analyze the impact of **pricing and promotional strategies on deodorant sales**, with a specific focus on **NIVEA** across major Dutch grocery chains.

---

## Project Structure

marketing_analytics_project/
│
├── dataset_deodorant.csv
│ Weekly retail dataset (2003–2006) including sales, prices, promotions, and chain information.
│
├── MARKETING.R
│ R script containing the full data preprocessing, exploratory analysis, and econometric modeling.
│
└── Marketing Analytics Project.pdf
Final academic report with methodology, results, and managerial insights.


---

## Summary

- Performed data cleaning and exploratory data analysis (EDA) in **R**.
- Analyzed price positioning, promotional intensity, and seasonality across brands and chains.
- Estimated pooled and unpooled regression models and applied **Chow tests**.
- Evaluated promotional effectiveness using linear, log-log, and multiplicative sales models.
- Identified chain-level heterogeneity and derived managerial recommendations.

---

## Usage

- Open `MARKETING.R` to reproduce the full analysis and modeling workflow.
- Use `dataset_deodorant.csv` as the input data for all analyses.
- Refer to `Marketing Analytics Project.pdf` for detailed explanations, results, and conclusions.
